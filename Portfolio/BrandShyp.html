Project Overview

BrandShyp.org is a multi-vendor e-commerce marketplace specifically designed for booking hiking tours in Sri Lanka. It connects travelers with local tour operators, offering a wide range of hiking experiences across Sri Lanka's diverse landscapes.

Key Features:

  • Multi-vendor platform allowing multiple tour operators to list their tours.
  • Destination-based browsing and tour discovery.
  • Detailed tour listings with itineraries, difficulty levels, and operator information.
  • Secure booking and payment processing.
  • Vendor dashboards for tour management and sales tracking.
  • Customer account management and booking history.
