Visit Live Website

Project Overview

Connect & Hike is a platform designed to connect hikers with local guides and trekking experts in Sri Lanka. It facilitates the process of finding and booking guided hiking experiences and adventures across the country.

Key Features:

  • Directory of hiking guides and trekking experts in Sri Lanka.
  • Guide profiles with experience, expertise, and contact information.
  • Search and filtering options to find guides based on location, expertise, and availability.
  • Contact forms for direct inquiries to guides.
  • Information and resources for planning hiking trips in Sri Lanka.
