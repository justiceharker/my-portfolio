Project Overview

hikelanka.com is a comprehensive multi-vendor marketplace platform dedicated to hiking tours in Sri Lanka. It connects travelers with a variety of tour operators, offering a diverse selection of hiking experiences across the island.

Key Features:

  • Multi-vendor e-commerce platform for hiking tours.
  • Extensive catalog of hiking tours in various Sri Lankan destinations.
  • Detailed tour listings with itineraries, photos, and operator information.
  • Advanced search and filtering options for tour discovery.
  • Secure online booking and payment processing.
  • Vendor dashboards for managing tours, bookings, and earnings.
  • Customer account management and booking history.
