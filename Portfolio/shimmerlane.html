Project Overview

Shimmer Lane is a website showcasing a curated collection of luxury villas and holiday homes available for rent in Sri Lanka. It aims to provide a platform for travelers seeking high-end accommodation and exceptional vacation experiences in Sri Lanka.

Key Features:

  • Showcase of luxury villas and holiday homes with detailed descriptions and high-quality imagery.
  • Destination-based browsing to find villas in specific locations in Sri Lanka.
  • Property details pages with amenities, availability, and contact information.
  • Contact forms for inquiries and booking requests.
  • Visually appealing design to highlight the luxury and beauty of the properties.
