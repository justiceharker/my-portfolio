Project Overview

Sri Lanka Export is an online directory platform designed to connect international buyers with Sri Lankan exporters and their products. It serves as a central hub for discovering and sourcing products from Sri Lanka across various industries.

Key Features:

  • Comprehensive directory of Sri Lankan exporters categorized by industry.
  • Exporter profiles with company information, product listings, and contact details.
  • Product listings showcasing Sri Lankan export goods.
  • Search and filtering options to find exporters and products efficiently.
  • Contact forms for buyers to connect with exporters directly.
  • Platform to promote Sri Lankan exports to a global audience.
